    Our Story

    Everything changes when you fix a goal and put a time stamp on it. It's an urge to create impact, to make a change. When ClareX was founded, we wanted to help businesses succeed in their core business processes while taking care of the rest work. It was a modest goal of moving forward impact and transformation. Today, after more than five years, we have grown from our sister concern, Lever & Gear, to offer a broader range of services from BPO, ITES, FInancing solutions to DIgital Marketing.

    • People, Planet and Sustenance

      Parameter to measure success? Is it the result or impact?

      In developing countries, the impact is not the primary driver, but the result with profit margins is. However, in this pursuit of delivering results, we have squandered our resources and workforce. This has delayed our transformational revolution, our hope for a bright future that could have brought record-breaking outputs.

    • Where should we head towards?

      The measure of success should combine economic, environmental, and social dimensions. A triple bottom line sustainability framework of 3 Ps People, Profit, and Planet, incorporates these dimensions and delivers impact. We believe in providing impact, CSRs that make a difference in society, create a place for everyone, and become an income source for the poor.

    • Triple Bottom Line (TBL), the feasible solution?

      Business is more than just generating a profit. Economic growth, environmental health, and social advancement are critical to long-term corporate success and sustainability. The Triple Bottom Line (TBL) philosophy is where we hold ourselves accountable in terms of Profit, Planet, and People. Furthermore, it is our constitutional commitment to safeguard and improve the ecosystem, biodiversity, and the earth environment for Bangladesh's current and future generations.

    • So, What we do at Clarex?

      At Clarex, we determine our performance in a broader sense by highlighting the sustainability and institutional growth that affects its surroundings spontaneously. This allows us to think differently while delivering the product/service. As each of our deliverables creates even the tiniest impact on society, the environment, we put together a strong accountability for profit, planet and People.
